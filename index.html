Lebanon Public Realm

Addressing issues and finding solutions to improve Lebanon's public realm.

Current Issues

(1) Lack of Safe Footpath

Description: Many areas in Lebanon lack proper footpaths. Drivers park their vehicles on the footpath, making it unsafe for pedestrians to walk.

Solution: Install bollards along footpaths to prevent illegal parking.

(2) Lack of End-to-End Public Transport

Description: Public transportation systems in Lebanon are fragmented and do not provide seamless connectivity. The Coastal Train is out of service, and no Park and Ride spaces are available.

Solution: Revive the Coastal Train and introduce Park and Ride facilities.

(3) Lack of Law Enforcement for Driving

Description: Traffic laws are often not enforced, leading to reckless driving and unsafe road conditions.

Solution: Increase traffic police presence and enforce penalties for violations.

(4) Poor Street Lighting

Description: Inconsistent street lighting. Some pedestrian areas do not have street lighting or the street lighting is not connected to an alternative power source.

Solution: Install solar-powered streetlights in poorly lit areas.

(5) Inadequate Waste Management

Description: No allowance for recycled bins.

Solution: Introduce recycling bins in public areas and educate citizens on waste segregation.

(6) Limited Green Spaces and Recreational Areas

Description: Developers are given permission to build every square foot of the cities, not enforcing the quota for green spaces.

Solution: Enforce zoning laws to allocate space for parks and recreational areas.

(7) Air and Noise Pollution in Urban Areas

Description: Drivers bump the horn at any time to voice their frustration of heavy traffic or if someone is using their phones and delay the flow of traffic.

Solution: Implement strict noise pollution laws and promote electric vehicles.
